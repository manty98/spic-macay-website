The Why

From Gross to Subtle

In a world bombarded by information, SPIC MACAY seeks to facilitate an awareness of the deeper and subtler values. Indian classical arts are not meant for mere entertainment; they are a tool for introspection.

The structure of the Raga, the stillness of Yoga, and the precision of Craft are designed to take the individual from the gross (visible) to the subtle (invisible). This is the "Intangible" aspect of our heritage—it cannot be seen, only felt.

Nishkam Seva

The Spirit of Service

Newton's third law states that for every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction. Conversely, there is the law of "Nishkama Karma": for every action in which there is no embedded expectation, there is a reaction that is far greater than the action.

This spirit of volunteering without looking for certificates or accolades is the heartbeat of SPIC MACAY.

Reflections from the Archive

Chintan

"Thought, Contemplation, and the Wisdom of the Masters"

“

Ustad Bismillah Khan

A memoir

Sitting in his small room in Varanasi, Khan Sahib once said: "I was playing the shehnai on the banks of the Ganges, when the Goddess Ganga rose out of the waters and blessed me."

He narrated this with such conviction and a glow in his eyes that I was moved to tears. For him, music was not performance; it was Ibadat (prayer). He used music to transcend form.
“

The Power of 'Sa'

Dr. Kiran Seth

When I asked Ustad Nasir Aminuddin Dagar to teach me, he made me sing the note 'Sa' for hours, everyday, for a month. He wouldn't even sit with me. I was frustrated.

Only later did the realization dawn: "Ek sade, sab saade" (Master one, master all). He wasn't teaching me a note; he was teaching me patience, focus, and the depth required to touch the alpha-zone.

“

Nishkama Karma

The Law of Action

Newton's third law can be experimentally proved. The law of Nishkama Karma can be proven experientially.

Check it out yourself. Do an act of service without telling anyone, without expecting a 'thank you'. The reaction comes from a source other than the one acted upon, and it brings a peace that is far greater than any material reward.
