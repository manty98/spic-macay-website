The Foundation

Four Pillars of
SPIC MACAY

Together working towards the collective effort of reaching every child and enriching the quality of education.

Pillar 01

Artists: "The Eye"

"Art washes away from the soul the dust of everyday life..."

Our artists take the Herculean challenge of linking our exquisite culture with the youth of our nation, elevating them to a level of serenity and glory. They form "THE EYE" of SPIC MACAY.

Without their undying support, the movement wouldn't survive a single day. Legends like Pt. Birju Maharaj, Ustad Bismillah Khan, and Pt. Bhimsen Joshi have considered performing for the youth as their way of giving back to society.

Pillar 02

Institutions

Institutions are the torchbearers. They play the role of inculcating the essence of our core Indian values in the psyche of every individual.

Starting with only IIT Delhi, SPIC MACAY has now partnered with thousands of schools and colleges, including Kendriya Vidyalayas and Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas. We are reaching children in rural areas and technical institutes alike, ensuring that heritage touches every corner of the educational landscape.

Pillar 03

Volunteers

"Research has shown that people who volunteer often live longer!"

Volunteers are the driving force, the skeleton, and the life of SPIC MACAY. From coordinating artists to managing the shoe rack at a venue, they do it all. They are self-driven individuals who undergo a paradigm shift through service, becoming refined individuals themselves.

It is their zeal that has brought the movement recognition from governments and corporates alike.

Service without expectation

Pillar 04

Supporters

Supporters are the backbone of SPIC MACAY. Without them, this colossal task would not be possible.

They enable us to move beyond geographical limitations and enlighten the world with Indian culture. Unique to this movement, our sponsors often end up becoming volunteers themselves, contributing with a large heart and going the extra mile to ensure we keep our promises to the youth.
