Our Philosophy

Not entertainment,
but enrichment.

SPIC MACAY (Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music And Culture Amongst Youth) is a non-political, nationwide voluntary movement founded in 1977 by Dr. Kiran Seth.

We believe that the abstract and subtle nature of Indian classical arts can help a student find the center within. It is about an inward journey, fostering patience, discipline, and a quiet mind in a noisy world.

Our intention is to enrich the quality of formal education by increasing awareness about different aspects of Indian heritage and inspiring the young mind to imbibe the values embedded in it.

The Cycle of Time

Indian Classical Music is not just art; it is a science of the soul. Melodies (Ragas) are prescribed for specific times of the day to align your inner rhythm with the movement of the sun.

Our Offerings

Core Activities

Virasat

Concerts

Live performances by maestros, bringing the magic of Raga and Tala directly to students.

Education

Talks & Lec-Dems

Interactive sessions where artists demystify the art form, explaining the grammar of music and dance.

Exploration

Heritage Walks

Guided walks through historical monuments and museums to connect with the history etched in stone.

Wellness

Yoga & Holistic Health

Practicing Hatha Yoga and Naad Yoga (Yoga of Sound) for physical and mental equilibrium.

Visual Art

Cinema Classics

Screenings of masterpieces by legends like Satyajit Ray, Kurosawa, and Bergman to refine visual literacy.

Intensives

Workshops

Intensive 5-day workshops with Gurus to learn the basics of classical art forms and crafts.
